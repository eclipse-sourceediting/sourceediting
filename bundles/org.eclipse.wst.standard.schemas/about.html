Open Mobile Alliance DTDs

The plug-in is accompanied by document type definitions ("WAP DTDs") defined by the Wireless Application Protocol (WAP) Forum. The WAP Forum has now been consolidated into the Open Mobile Alliance (OMA). The WAP DTDs are the following files located in the plug-in:

  • wml11.dtd obtained from http://www.wapforum.org/DTD/wml_1.1.xml
  • wml13.dtd obtained from http://www.wapforum.org/DTD/wml13.dtd
  • xhtml-mobile10-flat.dtd obtained from http://www.wapforum.org/DTD/xhtml-mobile10.dtd

Your use of the WAP DTDs is governed by the terms and conditions of the EPL.

More information about the WAP and the OMA is available at http://www.openmobilealliance.org/tech/affiliates/wap/wapindex.html.

W3C DTDs

The plug-in is accompanied by document type definitions (" W3C DTDs") defined by the the W3C Extensible HyperText Markup Language (XHTML) 1.1. XHTML is described at http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml11/.

The W3C DTDs are the following files located in the plug-in:

  • xhtml11-flat.dtd obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml11/DTD/xhtml11.dtd
  • xhtml1-frameset.dtd obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml1-frameset.dtd
  • xhtml1-strict obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml1-strict.dtd
  • xhtml1-transitional.dtd obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml1-transitional.dtd
  • xhtml-basic10-f.dtd obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml-basic/xhtml-basic10.dtd
  • xhtml-lat1.ent obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml-lat1.ent
  • xhtml-special.ent obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml-special.ent
  • xhtml-symbol.ent obtained from http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml-symbol.ent

As described in section 5.10 of the FAQ located at http://www.w3.org/Consortium/Legal/IPR-FAQ-20000620.html#DTD, your use of the W3C DTDs is governed by the terms and conditions of the W3C Software License a copy of which is included in the file about_files/copyright-software-19980720.htm and is also available at http://www.w3.org/Consortium/Legal/copyright-software-19980720.
